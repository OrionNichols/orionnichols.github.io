About Me

I am a mechanical engineer with a passion for robotics and aerospace

Interest and Extra Curriculars

As president of both the Aviation Club and ASME chapter, I led community focused outreach and organization while helping build a community of student leaders. In these roles, I helped oversee the installation of an Advanced Aviation Training Device,giving students and community members the opportunity to log hours in both their IFR and VFR flight times. We also ran courses for students interested in pursuing their part 107 drone certification, which I myself participated in and pursued myself. My goals post graduations include taking my part 107 exam and to pursue my pilots license.

In my personal time, I enjoy adventure, music, and boardgames. I studied violin for 14 years, transitioning to the guitar, which I have played for 12 years now. In my adventures I enjoy camping, hiking, and road biking. I enjoyed studying at Cal Poly Humboldt because it provided great access to beautiful outdoor adventures in the redwoods. I have been playing boardgames since I was very young, and more specifically I have enjoyed playing Dungeons and Dragons for about 20 years. I have run games for groups as small as three to as large as seven people, becoming a "forever dm" as it is known in tabletop spaces.

Check out my craft

I am an avid player of tabletop strategy games. I have been playing warhammer 40k for over five years. I began the hobby purely for mini painting which I have documented via my instagram. I have mostly played the game casually but have played at the tournament level.
